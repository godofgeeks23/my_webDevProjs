Lucknow Zoo

For the natural park in the city during your trip to Lucknow, you can visit Lucknow Zoo. It is also popular with the name of “Nawab Wazid Ali Shah Prani Udyan”, “Prince of Wales Zoological Gardens” and “Lucknow Zoological Garden”. You will get here 102 types of species and approximately 911 animals in the land area of 71.6 acres. This zoological park may be a good option while visiting with family or friends in Lucknow.

Bara Imambara

Bada Imambara or Bara Imambara is the most prominent historical spot that reminds the 18th century cultural and architectural effects. This great architecture was built by the Nawab of Awadh, Asaf-Ud-Daula and followed the Mughal Architecture. It is the faith that this Imambara has 1000 ways to climb the terrace but only one way to come back to the ground. And, it is also said that some ways reach the location under the Gomti River and various other locations near Lucknow and outer as well.

Ambedkar Memorial Park

Ambedkar Memorial Park is also famous for its structural and natural views. For things to do in Lucknow, this park may be one of the best options situated in Gomti Nagar. Formally known as Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar Samajik Parivartan Prateek Sthal, it is dedicated to the heroes who dedicated their lives to equality, humanity and social justice. During the one day trip of Lucknow, Ambedkar Park may be a good option whether traveling with your family or friends. You will see here 62 magnificent elephant statues, museum, Ambedkar Stupa, and various others.

Rumi Darwaza

Rumi Darwaza is located between the Bara Imambara and Chhota Imambara and also built by the Nawab Asaf-Ud-Daula. It is also famous as the Turkish Gate of Lucknow. And attracts visitors from each part of the city as well as the entire county. When coming from Chhota Imambara, the three distinct doorways welcome to you and increase its beauty.
